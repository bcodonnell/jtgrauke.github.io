About thirty percent of our Customer Support requests, surprisingly enough, have nothing to do with the software. Today alone we received about a dozen requests not really related to Populi at all. Here are some examples:

Hey, a bunch of us have been wondering what the Populi staff would look like with Bieber-hair. Can you guys jump on that? Thank you for giving priority attention to this matter.

Our 13-year-old registrar has a very important Feature Request: the Populi staff should all get Justin Bieber haircuts. Thanks for all your hard work!

Hey, what do you guys look like with Justin Bieber hair? Please advise.

Populi's great and all, but... I just feel like I'm NEVER gonna see what you guys look like with awesome Justin Bieber hair :(

Well, folks, all we can say is... Never Say Never. A friend slipped us some SuperCuts coupons and we took the plunge... because Customer Support is the backbone of what we do.
